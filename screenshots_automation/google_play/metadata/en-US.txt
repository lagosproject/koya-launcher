Title: Koya - Minimal Launcher
========================================
Short Description:
A clean, focused launcher featuring a custom widget slot and quick search.
========================================
Full Description:
Koya Launcher is a minimalist, distraction-free Android launcher designed to help you regain focus, reduce screen time, and customize your phone for productivity. 

By replacing your standard home screen with a clean, text-based interface, Koya eliminates visual clutter, notification badges, and the constant urge to mindlessly scroll.

Key Features:
• Minimalist Home Screen: A clean, distraction-free interface featuring a simple date & time header.
• Dedicated Widget Slot: Long-press the top section to host your favorite, essential widget (e.g., calendar, task manager, or weather).
• 4 Custom Text Shortcuts: Pin your four most-used apps at the bottom of the screen as clean text links.
• Smart App Drawer: Access all your apps instantly. The built-in search features pre-computed diacritical mark normalization for lag-free, instant filtering.
• Screen Usage & Calendar: Optional integrations to view your screen time and upcoming calendar events directly on the home screen.
• Battery Indicator: A subtle, clean battery level bar to keep you informed without clutter.
• Privacy-First: Koya operates entirely offline. It does not require internet permission, meaning your data never leaves your device. No ads, no tracking.
